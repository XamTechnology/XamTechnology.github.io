WHAT IS XAM?

A platform for collaborative learning where people can manage, share and discuss their study materials.

Study Material Management

Be more productive with organized study materials. Manage your notes for each subject and keep them safe. You can also get data collected from all your notes.

Active Online Community

Discover people in the same courses as you. Add and chat with them to compare your notes and study materials. You can even create chat groups for those in the same classes.

Web & Mobile Access

Study on the go. XAM and your study materials will be there for you. You don't have to bring all those books and papers. All this is accessible through a few clicks from your phone and computers.
